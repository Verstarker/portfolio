About Safe House

Safe House was the first project I did at Yoobee. Its main focus was on the UX process leading towards development.

The research phase started with a kick-off meeting with the client. They wanted to promote a new app in development. They also wanted design-wise to keep the same colours from their own site but had the choice to add in something new. This was where we got the basic idea of the clients needs. As well as kick-off meetings, I completed some card-sorting and surveys. From this, I had an idea of what information I would need to display, the target audience, and design ideas.

The design phase was completed by creating a mixture of wireframes, style guides and mood boards. From those, I created a high fidelity design in Adobe Photoshop which would be the basis of the site I would develop.

The site itself was developed as a static website using only HTML and CSS. The design used a lot of blue tones from the parent company but with an additional teal colour.
